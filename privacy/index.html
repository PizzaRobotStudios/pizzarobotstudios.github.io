Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 29, 2026

No accounts required across any of our apps. No email, login, or personal info collected. Anonymous analytics and crash reporting help us improve our games. Free versions show ads via Google AdMob. We never sell your personal information.

Pizza Robot Studios LLC ("we," "us," "our") is an independent game studio based in Los Angeles, California. This Privacy Policy explains how our applications ("Apps") handle information when you use them. This policy covers all current and future apps published by Pizza Robot Studios LLC.

Our Apps

This policy applies to all games and applications published by Pizza Robot Studios LLC, including but not limited to Rock Paper PvP and future titles.

Information You Provide

Our Apps may allow you to choose a player name and avatar icon. These are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to our servers unless a shared leaderboard feature is active, in which case your chosen player name and game stats may be visible to other players. Please do not use personal information in your player name.

Information Collected Automatically

Our Apps may automatically collect certain information to help us understand how our games are used and to maintain stability. This may include:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Anonymous usage events
  • Crash logs and error reports
  • Approximate location derived from IP address (depending on ad and analytics settings)

This data is anonymous and is not used to personally identify you.

Third-Party Services

Our Apps may use the following third-party services, each of which may collect information as described in their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob — Displays ads in free versions. Ad partners may use device identifiers to serve relevant ads based on your settings and region.
  • Firebase Analytics — Collects anonymous usage data to help us understand how our Apps are used and what features matter most.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — Collects crash reports and diagnostic data to help us identify and fix bugs quickly.
  • App Store Purchases — Premium upgrade purchases are processed by Apple or Google. We never receive your payment card details.

How We Use Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Apps
  • Measure gameplay performance and improve the user experience
  • Diagnose crashes, bugs, and technical issues
  • Display ads in free versions (disabled for Premium users)

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited anonymous data with service providers such as ad networks and analytics platforms solely to operate our Apps and measure performance. These providers are bound by their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through one of our Apps, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

Your Choices

You have control over your experience with our Apps:

  • You can limit ad tracking and personalization through your device's privacy settings.
  • You can choose not to enter any personal information in player names.
  • You can purchase Premium to disable advertisements.
  • You can delete any App at any time to remove all locally stored data from your device.

Data Retention

Player data such as names, avatars, and game progress is stored locally on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App or reset your progress in Settings. Anonymous analytics and crash data is retained by our third-party service providers according to their standard retention policies.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the limited data associated with our Apps. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of any information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our Apps after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, contact us at support@pizzarobotstudios.com.
